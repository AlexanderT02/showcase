PRIMA Insights

Internal sales intelligence platform used by a hospitality supplier to activate dormant customer demand and structure follow-ups across the sales team.

Built a sales activation system that turns passive shop behavior into assigned follow-up actions. Reduced manual lead hunting and enabled agents to systematically recover missed revenue opportunities.

Problem

Shop behavior data existed, but it was not operationally accessible for sales. Follow-ups relied on manual communication, personal memory and inconsistent routines.

Goal: convert passive events into assigned, low-noise actions that integrate into a weekly workflow.

Approach

PRIMA Insights converts behavioral signals into assigned follow-up actions. Relevant activity triggers lead creation, ownership assignment and automated weekly activation emails.

This turns passive shop activity into a consistent follow-up pipeline instead of isolated analytics.

Impact

  • Used weekly by the sales team
  • Replaced manual follow-up workflows
  • Turned shop analytics into assigned leads
  • Centralized customer interaction visibility

Core Flow

Weekly Lead Activation

Automated weekly emails highlight customer interest without purchase. Each email links directly to the dashboard, where agents can filter assigned companies and review detailed activity before initiating follow-up.

Signal Interpretation

The main challenge was avoiding noise: most shop activity represents normal purchasing behavior, not sales opportunities. The system therefore focuses on detecting unfulfilled demand rather than activity itself.

Regular orders and routine re-purchases are filtered out, while interactions without conversion are interpreted as potential interest.

Lead Qualification

Behavioral signals are evaluated and reduced to a small set of relevant follow-ups. Instead of exposing every event, the platform produces qualified lead candidates that can be reviewed and owned by a responsible agent.

This keeps the system useful for sales rather than overwhelming them with analytics data.

Operational Pipeline

Incoming activity passes through a stable processing flow: ingest → filter routine behavior → detect unmet interest → assign responsibility → notify → review.

The weekly reminder acts as a lightweight trigger, integrating the system into a consistent sales routine.

System Context

Production microservice architecture composed of a core processing service and a dedicated web application backend.

The processing service performs data transformation, aggregation and automated email delivery, while the web backend handles authentication, RBAC and dashboard interaction.
