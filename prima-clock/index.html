PRIMA Clock

Production system used by internal teams for operational and financial accuracy.

Internal production system for tracking and validating billable work across clients, projects and departments. Serves as the operational basis for cost allocation and invoicing.

Purpose

Time tracking without customer linkage creates unreliable billing. PRIMA Clock enforces responsibility by connecting every session to a client and project before it can be recorded.

Design Approach

The system models responsibility explicitly: users, projects and customers are relationally linked, enabling filtered analytics, department comparison and billing validation workflows.

Impact

  • Used daily across departments
  • Created verifiable billing records
  • Centralized project-based cost allocation

Core Flow

Daily Workflow

Start and stop timers, add notes and switch projects — sessions become verifiable billing records.
