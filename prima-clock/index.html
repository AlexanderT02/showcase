PRIMA Clock

Internal operations software for tracking billable work across clients, projects and departments. Built as a production system for daily business use at Prima GmbH.

Core Flow

Daily Workflow

Start and stop timers, add notes and switch projects — sessions become verifiable billing records.

Purpose

Companies often track time, but rarely track it in a way that connects employees, projects and customers reliably. PRIMA Clock was developed to create a single source of truth for billable work. Every recorded session can be reviewed, corrected and analyzed before invoicing.

Design Approach

Instead of tracking attendance, the system tracks responsibility. Work is always connected to a customer and project, enabling transparent cost allocation, department comparison and reliable operational decisions.
